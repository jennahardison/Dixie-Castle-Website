Lunch Menu


For lunch, Dixie Castle is mostly known for their plate lunches. Every day there is a different plate lunch, but thats not the only thing the serve during lunch. Dixie Castle also serves burgers, sandwiches, salads, and some items off the dinner menu. The desserts are also the same as what they serve during dinner hours.
