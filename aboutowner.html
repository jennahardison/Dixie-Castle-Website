About the Owner

Mark Hudgens has been the owner of Dixie Castle since April of 1989. Hudgens is also the current owner of Dixie Castle, Dixie II, and Dixie Airways. Mark opened Dixie II in 2004 and Dixie Airways in 2014. Dixie Castle is a family owned business. Mark’s wife, Gina Hudgens, occasionally waits tables at Dixie Castle. Hudgens’ two daughters, Jessica Hudgens and Brandy Hudgens also waitressed at Dixie Castle when they were younger.
