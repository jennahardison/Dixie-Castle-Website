About the Owner

Mark Hudgens is the current owner of Dixie Castle. Mark has been the owner for over 20 years. Since becoming the owner of Dixie Castle, Hudgens has opened two other locations. Mark is one of the main cooks at Dixie Castle along with two other employees. Mark's wife, Gina Hudgens, also works at Dixie Castle as a waitress from time to time. When they were younger, Mark's daughters were also waitresses at Dixie Castle.
